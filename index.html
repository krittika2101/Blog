A short glimpse about Singapore

Singapore is a small nation with a population of only 5.7 million people. Being widely known as a little red dot on the world map, this does not limit Singapore in terms of it's beauty!

From places of nature, to the closely knitted bond of people of different races and religions, and the diversity of culture, there is much more to explore and learn here.

While having developed as a metropolitan city, Singapore is widely known for for being a clean and green city. In fact, Singapore's biggest wealth would be our trees and greenery, as well as our racial harmony
